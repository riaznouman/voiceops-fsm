# Technician Persona

## Name
Jake Mitchell

## Role
Field Service Technician

## Age
28

## Background
Jake has been working as a field technician for 3 years. He handles on-site repairs, device pickups, and deliveries across the Brisbane metro area. He's comfortable with smartphones and apps but doesn't consider himself tech-savvy. He prefers simple, clear interfaces that let him get things done quickly between jobs.

## Goals
- View his daily job list quickly without scrolling through menus
- Get directions to the next job location with one tap
- Update job status on the go (en route, arrived, in progress, completed)
- Add notes and photos from the field
- Log his hours and expenses at the end of the day

## Frustrations
- Too many taps to find basic information
- Apps that are slow on mobile data
- Having to call the office to get job details or customer phone numbers
- Forgetting to update job status and getting calls from the manager

## Device & Context
- Uses a mid-range Android phone (Samsung Galaxy A series)
- Often works in areas with spotty mobile coverage
- Needs to use the app with one hand while carrying tools
- Wears work gloves sometimes, so big tap targets are important

## Key Scenarios
1. Morning: Opens app to see today's jobs and the first address
2. Between jobs: Marks current job as completed, checks next job details
3. On-site: Looks up customer contact info, adds repair notes
4. End of day: Reviews completed jobs, submits daily timesheet
